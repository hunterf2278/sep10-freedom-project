I have finally finished my research and learned about the different softwares and hardware that writers can use. For example, one  I found was [Scrivener](https://scrivener.app/).  I found it by searching "Writing apps."  Scrivener has a lot of helpful features like letting you organize your writing, publish or export your work, and even track how many  times you use certain words so you donâ€™t repeat yourself too much. Another website I discovered by searching "What are some software that writers use for writing?" was the website [Dabblewriter](https://www.dabblewriter.com/). This site allows you to plan out the different parts of your story, which makes it easier to remember what happens next. It can also read your writing out loud so you can catch your mistakes or awkward sentences. Plus, you can leave yourself notes as you write, which is great for remembering changes you want to make later. 

Now, in order to find different hardwares, I searched in google "Writing hardware" and found a webite called [Distraction-Free-Writing-Devices](https://kadavy.net/distraction-free-writing-devices/) which helped me learn about many diiferent types. One hardware was the [Freewrite](https://getfreewrite.com/?srsltid=AfmBOopuxMTi52KrlPe-I2sVPK2BBgXrVgg_jrMpEmKl-yApBsx6fI6Q). One function of this device that stood out to me was that the device display is concentration friendly and visually comfortable, unlike computer screens which can strain the eye. Another function is that this device has a longer lasting battery so you can write for longer periods of time. Lastly, there are many types of Freewrite's, such as a Freewrite Traveler, Freewrite Smart Typewriter, and Freewrite Alpha. The other hardware I reasearched was the [BYOK](https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/byok/byok-the-ultimate-distraction-free-writing-tool). This was my favorite device because it has tools to improve your writing virtually, and physically. For instance, the BYOK has no apps, no notifactions, and no distractions which allows people to focus more on their writing. The BYOK isn't physcially attached to a computer, you can move it into any viewing postion that's comfortable for you, and if your comfortable then you can have more fun writing while relaxing. Finally, the Byok has a portable and compact design that is easy to carry, this allows you to take your work and inspiration with you wherever you want to go.
